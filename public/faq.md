## What is Shopify CRO?
Shopify Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) is the practice of increasing the percentage of visitors who buy. It includes PDP redesign, cart and checkout improvements, trust and urgency systems, A/B testing, and analytics. The Heroes Agency runs CRO sprints that have moved client conversion rates from 1.4% to 3.1% in 90 days. https://www.theheroesagency.org/shopify-optimization.

## What is Shopify speed optimization?
Speed optimization improves Core Web Vitals — Largest Contentful Paint (LCP), Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS), Interaction to Next Paint (INP). Faster Shopify stores rank better on Google and convert more visitors. Typical wins: image optimization, deferred apps, theme refactor, server-side tracking.
